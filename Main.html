Apollo Group

Apollo is the 1st group of the ITMAWE 102 section. Our section was divided into 3 groups, Apollo was the 1st group of the section it consists of 11 members, our current leader is Joshua Ocbina, he makes sure that we are all working together when there's iLS projects.

What is iLS?

iLS stands for "i Learn and Share" this is the main project for our every subjects. The main goal of iLS is to make sure that every student can communicate to their fellow student, because the reason of iLS is to bring harmony to every student. iLS wants us to learn the power of teamwork and friendship.
